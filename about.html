About Me

I am a recent graduate in Nutrition and Food Science with a specialization in Nutrition and Wellness, and currently pursuing a Master of Science in Engineering Management at Cumberland University. I am deeply motivated by the intersection of science, management, and wellness — combining technical insight with human-centered care.

Through my work as a Direct Support Personnel (DSP) at Homecare Preference LLC and True Vision Services Inc., I developed individualized nutrition plans, promoted healthy habits, and supported individuals with disabilities in achieving independence and comfort.

My goal is to empower communities to embrace better health practices through evidence-based nutrition, effective communication, and compassionate leadership.
